Overview

This privacy policy describes how Mohammad Shaban ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with personal projects, portfolio applications, and tools I build and maintain. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about my data practices.

Information I Collect

Depending on which application or tool you use, I may collect:

  • Basic profile information from social platforms (name, username, profile picture) when you connect an account via OAuth
  • Usage data such as posts, schedules, and content you create within the application
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, and device type for security and debugging purposes
  • Contact information (name, email) if you submit a contact form

How I Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide and operate the application features you have requested
  • Authenticate you with connected social media platforms on your behalf
  • Publish content to social platforms at your direction
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the applications

Third-Party Services

My applications may integrate with third-party platforms including X (Twitter), LinkedIn, Instagram, and others. When you connect these accounts, you authorise the application to act on your behalf according to the permissions you grant. Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data. I do not sell or share your data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely and access is limited to what is necessary to operate the application. OAuth tokens used to connect social accounts are encrypted at rest. I retain your data only for as long as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your social accounts at any time through the application settings
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Cookies

Applications may use session cookies strictly necessary for authentication and security. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Changes to This Policy
